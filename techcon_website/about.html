History

TechCon was founded in 2010 with the goal of providing a space for technology professionals to network, share knowledge, and discuss innovations in the tech industry. Over the years, TechCon has grown into one of the largest tech conferences globally, featuring keynote speakers, workshops, and discussions on the latest trends in technology.

Our Mission

TechCon 2024 is driven by the mission to foster collaboration, innovation, and education in the technology field. Our aim is to empower individuals and organizations to embrace new technologies and drive change in the world.

Notable Past Speakers

TechCon has been honored to host some of the brightest minds in the tech industry. These speakers have shared their expertise, insights, and vision for the future of technology:

Elon Musk

CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, Elon Musk delivered a groundbreaking keynote on the future of AI and space exploration in 2019.

Sundar Pichai

As CEO of Google, Sundar Pichai spoke in 2020 about the role of AI and cloud computing in reshaping the tech landscape.

Sheryl Sandberg

Facebook's former COO, Sheryl Sandberg, shared her insights on leadership and diversity in tech during her keynote in 2021.
